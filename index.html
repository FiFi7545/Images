2025–26 pay award ranges

Awards must fall within the permitted range for each BYB rating band. Awards outside these ranges require prior HRBP and Director approval.

Rating Increase range
Underperforming 0% — not eligible for an increase
Striving 1% – 1.5%  (max 1.5%)
Achieving 2% – 3%  (max 3%)
Exceeding 3.1% – 3.5%  (max 3.5%)
Outstanding 3.5%+  (subject to budget; discuss with HRBP)

A few things to keep in mind

  Your overall team budget is 3% of the combined salary of all eligible employees in your team. The spreadsheet will show your running spend as you enter figures.
  CIPD is committed to paying our people at a minimum of 80% of their salary range. The spreadsheet will flag anyone below this threshold. Contact the People team if the required uplift is above what your budget allows.
  If you override the suggested overall rating, please email the People team with the employee's name and both the suggested and applied ratings.
  Do not discuss salary changes with your team before 1 July 2026. Employees who have resigned or received an end of contract letter before 1 July are not eligible for a pay review increase.
